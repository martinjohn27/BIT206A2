Swim Paradise has a good-sized outdoor salted water pool, allowing ample space for two to three dogs to play each session. Well-equipped as well, Swim Paradise has a shower area, dryer (for both dogs and owners), a little garden for dogs, cute animal floats, and a cafe. Swim Paradise is a favourite among many dog owners thanks to their great service, cleanliness, and well-maintained dog swimming pool and facilities.Do book ahead of time as this popular place fills up fast. Swimming your dog is a perfect activity for the young or old. Swimming is a non-weight bearing activity that avoids all the stresses and strains of exercising on hard ground. Our pool water is generated in salt water chlorination process.
